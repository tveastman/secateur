Blocking accounts using secateur

Secateur blocks a user and all their followers, and it can automatically unblock them later after a time of your choosing. If secateur is under heavy load, then it can take a while for blocking to start.

There's a rate-limit that affects how many people you can block. It works just like a stamina bar in a game: it refills slowly over time up to a maximum. The maximum and the speed at which it refills are things I tune every once in a while depending on how overloaded the server is.

Important note: If you change your Twitter screen name, Secateur won't work for you anymore until you log in again. The credentials Secateur users are tied to your Twitter username. If you change your screen name secateur can't unblock or block users for you until you log into to secateur with your new screen name.

Muting people is way, way slower than blocking them, and you can't mute many people. I recommend just using 'block' and 'block followers' and not using mute at all on secateur (I intend to move mute to its own page later).
